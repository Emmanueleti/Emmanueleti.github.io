Our Facilities

Modern library, advanced labs, sports field, and vibrant cafeteria—spaces designed to fuel learning, innovation, and campus life

World class Library

Our three-story library offers 150,000+ print and digital resources, 24/7 study zones with charging stations, and dedicated research support from expert librarians. The silent reading rooms and group collaboration areas cater to all learning styles, while interlibrary loan services provide access to global academic collections.

Largest Play Ground

Our NBA-standard outdoor court features professional shock-absorbing flooring and LED lighting for night games. Home to varsity tournaments and weekend coaching clinics, the echoing squeaks and roaring crowds make it the energetic heart of campus athletics.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The airy, solar-powered dining hall serves 2,000+ meals daily, featuring live cooking stations, allergen-free zones, and locally sourced ingredients. Flexible meal plans accommodate diverse diets, while the adjacent lounge with courtyard views creates a vibrant social hub between classes.
